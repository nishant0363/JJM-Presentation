The Jal Jeevan Mission Research Program at IIMB addresses critical issues of rural drinking water in India. We have produced effective mappings and analysis of Jal Jeevan Water Quality data.

Our comprehensive water quality report includes data from over 7 lakh villages across India, encompassing around 2 million habitations. Covering water quality data for more than 30 major pollutants over 15 years (2009-2024), it is continuously updated with current data.

This website presents our work on the tabular analysis and mappings generated from the Jal Jeevan data.
